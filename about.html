My Journey

I started my journey in tech with curiosity and a simple “Hello World.” Since then, I’ve explored programming in C and built beginner-friendly web projects using HTML and CSS. Every project — from a profile card to a timetable — has helped me understand how structure, logic, and design come together. I’m learning to present my work professionally through GitHub and live demos, and I take pride in building things that are honest, accessible, and skill-matched.

Beyond Coding

Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring design ideas, organizing my projects, and improving how users interact with my portfolio. I also find inspiration in music and quiet moments — they help me think clearly and stay creative when I return to the screen.
